Everything we have is a gift from God, and giving is simply our glad response — a way of saying that our hearts, and not only our wallets, belong to him. We never want giving to feel like a bill to pay. It is an act of worship, and it is always a joy, never a pressure.

## Where your giving goes

Your generosity keeps the lights on and the doors open, but it does far more than that. It supports the pastors and staff who care for our family, funds the ministries that welcome children and students, and reaches beyond our walls to serve neighbors in need and partners around the world. When you give, you are part of all of it.

## A word to our guests

If you're visiting for the first time, please don't feel any obligation to give. You are our guest, and giving is for those who call this church home. Simply enjoy being with us.

## How to give

You can give securely online through our giving partner using the button below. You're also welcome to give in person during either Sunday service. If you'd like to set up regular giving or have questions about how it works, our office is always happy to help.

Thank you for partnering with us. "God loves a cheerful giver," and we are grateful for every one of you.
