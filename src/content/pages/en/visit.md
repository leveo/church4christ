We're so glad you're thinking about visiting Church4Christ. Walking into a new church for the first time can feel like a big step, so here's everything you need to know before you arrive. However you come — curious, cautious, or somewhere in between — there is a seat saved for you.

## When we gather

We hold two Sunday services. The 9:30 AM service is in English, and the 11:00 AM service is in Chinese, with the same warm welcome at both. Most people arrive ten minutes early to grab a coffee and find a seat. A typical service lasts a little over an hour and includes singing, prayer, and a message from the Bible.

If Sunday mornings don't work for your schedule, our Wednesday evening prayer gathering at 7:00 PM is another gentle place to begin.

## What to expect

Come as you are — you'll see everything from sweaters to Sunday best, and no one will look twice. When you arrive, a greeter near the front doors will help you find your way and answer any questions. You don't need to bring anything, sign anything, or know anyone. You're welcome simply to sit, listen, and see.

We won't single you out or ask you to stand up. If you'd like to say hello afterward, we'd love that; if you'd rather slip out quietly, that's completely fine too.

## For your kids

Children are a joy to us, not a distraction. Our check-in table opens thirty minutes before each service. We'll print a name tag for your child and a matching tag for you, so only you can pick them up afterward. Age-appropriate classes run during the message, and infants are welcome in our staffed nursery. If your child would rather stay with you in the service, that's welcome too.

## Finding us

We meet at 123 Grace Avenue, with free parking in the lot behind the building and additional street parking nearby. Look for the welcome banner at the main entrance. If you get turned around, call the church office and someone will walk you in.

We can't wait to meet you.
