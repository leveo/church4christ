title: Welcome to Our New Website
author: Pastor David Chen
date: "2026-01-11"
excerpt: A few words about why we built this new home online, and how we hope it serves you and our neighbors.
---

For years, finding us online meant squinting at an outdated page with the wrong service times and a phone number that hadn't worked since before the pandemic. So we're glad to finally welcome you to our new website — a simpler, warmer front door to our church family.

We built it with two people in mind. The first is the neighbor who has driven past our building a hundred times and finally, quietly, wants to know what happens inside. If that's you, we hope you'll find everything you need to take a first step: when we gather, what to expect, and how we'll care for your kids.

The second is you, our family, who keep this church running week after week. Here you'll find the weekly bulletin, sermons to catch up on, and the ministries where you serve, all in one place and in both of our languages.

> "How good and pleasant it is when God's people live together in unity." — Psalm 133:1

A website is only ever a doorway, never the house itself. The real church is the people — praying, singing, carrying one another's burdens, and pointing each other back to Jesus. But we hope this doorway is a welcoming one. Come on in.
