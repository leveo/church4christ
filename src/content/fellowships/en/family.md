The Family Fellowship is a gathering for couples and parents in the thick of raising children and building a home. We meet on Saturday afternoons in the Fellowship Hall, where the grown-ups can share life while the kids play safely nearby.

Parenting can be joyful and lonely at the same time. Here you'll find other families who understand the tired seasons, the small victories, and the questions that keep you up at night. We share meals, study Scripture, and pray for one another's marriages and children.

Bring the whole family, noise and all. This is a place where children are expected, not merely tolerated, and where no one has to have it all figured out.
