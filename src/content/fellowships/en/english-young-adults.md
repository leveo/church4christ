English Young Adults is a community for those who do life mostly in English — including many who grew up in Chinese-speaking homes and now find themselves between two worlds. We meet after the Sunday services over coffee in the Café Corner, no formality required.

For a lot of us, faith and culture and family are tangled together in complicated ways. This is a place to untangle them honestly, in English, among friends who get it. We read Scripture, share what the week actually held, and figure out together what it looks like to follow Jesus here and now.

If you've ever felt like you didn't quite fit in either the English or the Chinese congregation, this might be exactly your corner. Pull up a chair.
