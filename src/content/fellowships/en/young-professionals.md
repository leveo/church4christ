The Young Professionals Fellowship is a home for working adults navigating the busy, often disorienting years of early career and adulthood. We meet on Friday evenings to catch our breath at the end of the week, share a meal, and open the Bible together.

Our evenings are simple: food, a time in Scripture, and honest conversation in small groups. We talk about the things this season actually raises — ambition and burnout, singleness and dating, money, calling, and how to keep faith at the center when work wants all of you.

You don't need to come with answers or even much energy. Come as you are on a Friday night. There's a seat, a warm meal, and a group of friends walking the same road.
