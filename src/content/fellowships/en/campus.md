The Campus Fellowship is for university and college students, many of them far from home for the first time. We meet on Thursday evenings — a midweek anchor in a schedule of lectures, deadlines, and late nights.

The student years are a season of big questions: Who am I? What is true? What will I do with my one life? We don't rush past those questions or hand out easy answers. We study the Bible honestly, wrestle with doubt out loud, and build the kind of friendships that outlast graduation.

Whether you grew up in church or have never opened a Bible, whether your faith is strong or barely a flicker, there's a place for you here. Come hungry — there's always food.
