The Seniors Fellowship is an unhurried, warm gathering for the older members of our family. We meet on Wednesday mornings, when the building is quiet and there's no rush to be anywhere else.

Ours is a season with its own gifts and its own griefs — more time and more memories, but also failing health, empty chairs, and the ache of loss. We meet these things together, over tea and Scripture, with laughter and with prayer. There is deep wisdom in this room, and we are richer for listening to it.

If you or your parents would enjoy an unhurried morning among friends who understand this stage of life, you are warmly welcome. Transport can be arranged — just ask.
