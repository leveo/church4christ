Emily keeps the whole operation running, and she does it with a calm that the rest of us find frankly enviable. As church administrator she looks after the calendar, the communications, the finances, and the hundred small logistics that let ministry happen without anyone tripping over the details.

If you've emailed the church office, booked a room, received the weekly newsletter, or wondered how everything somehow ends up in the right place at the right time — that's Emily. She is organized without being rigid, and she has a gift for saying "yes, and here's how" far more often than "no."

Emily came to the role from a background in nonprofit administration, and she sees her work as genuine ministry rather than mere paperwork. She is the friendly, capable first point of contact for our whole church family — and usually the reason things run on time.
