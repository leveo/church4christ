Sarah joined our pastoral team to help carry the ministry of preaching and teaching, and she has quickly become one of the voices our congregation most loves to learn from. She has a gift for making the hardest passages of Scripture feel both clear and alive, and for reminding us that good theology should always end in worship.

She came to faith as a university student through a campus ministry, an experience that gave her a lifelong burden for students and young adults finding their footing. She trained at seminary before joining us, and she still teaches with the energy of someone who can't quite believe she gets to do this for a living.

Sarah loves long-distance running, strong coffee, and any book that keeps her up too late. She is quick to laugh, slow to give up on people, and always ready for a good conversation.
