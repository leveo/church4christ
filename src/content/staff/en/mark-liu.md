Mark serves as an elder, sharing the work of leading, teaching, and caring for our church family. He came to faith as an adult, out of a season of real doubt, and that journey left him with an unusual patience for questions and for people who aren't sure they belong yet.

He co-leads the Young Professionals Fellowship, where his own story — from skeptic to disciple — gives him a natural bridge to those weighing the claims of Christ for the first time. Mark believes the church should be the safest place in the world to ask an honest question, and he works to make it so.

In his working life Mark is an accountant, which the congregation gently teases him about; he insists there is real joy in bringing order to a spreadsheet. He and his family are steady, generous, and deeply woven into the life of the church.
