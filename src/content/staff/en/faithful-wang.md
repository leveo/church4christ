Faithful has been part of Church4Christ almost since the beginning, and his life among us has more than lived up to his name. As an elder, he helps shepherd the congregation, guard its teaching, and pray for its people — often long before anyone knows there is anything to pray about.

By trade he spent his career as a schoolteacher, and he still carries a teacher's patience and a teacher's eye for the person at the edge of the room. He leads our Seniors Fellowship and has a particular tenderness for those walking through grief and old age.

Faithful and his wife are fixtures of the church's quiet backbone: first to arrive, last to leave, and always ready to put the kettle on for anyone who needs to talk.
