Grace oversees the fellowships and pastoral care that hold our church family together through every season of life. If you've been welcomed into a small group, comforted in a hospital waiting room, or gently followed up with after a hard Sunday, there's a good chance Grace was somewhere behind it.

Her own faith was shaped in a fellowship group much like the ones she now leads, and she has never forgotten what it meant to be known and prayed for as a newcomer. She writes and teaches with warmth and honesty, especially on prayer and the ordinary disciplines of walking with God.

Grace has a deep love for the Psalms, a well-worn journal she prays through each morning, and an open door that always seems to have room for one more at the table.
